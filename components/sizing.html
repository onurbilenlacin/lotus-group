Easily make an element as wide or as tall (relative to its parent) with our width and height utilities. Includes support for25% ,50% ,75% , and100% by default.

Width
.w-100
.w-75
.w-50
.w-25

<div class="bg-light p-2 border-dashed mb-3 w-75"><code>.w-75</code></div>
<div class="bg-light p-2 border-dashed mb-3 w-50"><code>.w-50</code></div>
<div class="bg-light p-2 border-dashed mb-3 w-25"><code>.w-25</code></div>
